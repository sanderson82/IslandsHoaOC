Continue to allow video access to board meetings because it increases transparency and allows more homeowners to participate in the decision-making process. Providing video access to meetings allows homeowners who cannot attend the meetings in person to still stay informed and participate in the discussions. It also promotes accountability and trust between the board and the homeowners by allowing them to see the actions and decisions being made on their behalf.

A website for would be a valuable addition as it provides a central hub for all important information, such as upcoming events, meetings, and important deadlines, as well as community guidelines and bylaws. Additionally, it can foster a sense of community and engagement among residents by allowing them to communicate with each other and the board, and make payments and access records.
